Manifesto

Fine Mess is a creative studio founded
on integrity of creativity.

Because too often we hear ‘no’ through fear.
Ideas and innovation wilt because of ‘what if?’

Watered down to dry out. Ships that
never leave safe harbour.

Well for us idea is king.

Not process.
Not parent.
Not procurement
or placement or palette.

Idea.

We’re here to smash champagne against
their hulls. And all our energy goes towards
making them better.

We’re in the business of creative direction,
not creative protection. Acceptable is unacceptable.

creative returns the most profit. Not to mention pride. So we craft the things that scare us a little.

design, music, merch, movies… You name it,
we’ll fame it.

Because people deserve it. And if we don't know how we’ll learn. Because done is better than perfect. Then we perfect that.

That’s Fine Mess. Not a style but a standard.
A mantra, a method and a mission.
